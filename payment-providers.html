--- title: Payment Providers heading: Payment Providers subtitle: The companies we authorise to take payments on our behalf description: Payment providers authorised to take payments on behalf of Vantibyte ---

There are currently two providers authorised to process payments for Vantibyte.

Stripe

Used for recurring subscriptions billed via credit/debit card, or one-off invoiced payments.
Statements should clearly show VANTIBYTE and may include STRIPE*

GoCardless

Used for recurring subscriptions billed via direct debit from a bank account.
Unfortunately, the wording on your statement may vary:

  • Some UK Banks will only show GoCardless Ltd
  • Other UK Banks will show GOCARDLESS-VANTIBYTE
  • Certain European banks will show GVANTIBYTE
  • Worldwide Banks may show differently, depending on their payment infrastructure

If you have any questions about a charge to your account containing our name, please get in touch.
